About Aminoac Town

Aminoac Town is one of the oldest and most vibrant communities in the NewNanCity Server. Founded in 2020, we have grown over the years to become one of the most prosperous towns on the server.

Located at the picturesque junction of forests and mountains, our town boasts unique natural scenery and well-developed infrastructure. We focus on community building and encourage residents to help each other, creating a wonderful gaming experience together.

The town features medieval-style architecture, modern agricultural zones, automated redstone farms, an impressive underground transportation network, and a bustling commercial center. Whether you're a building enthusiast, a redstone engineer, or an explorer, there's a place for you here.

We regularly organize community events such as building competitions, treasure hunts, and holiday celebrations, ensuring every resident feels the warmth and joy of our community.
